<p><ul><li>Neurovascular Embolectomy Device</li><li>Embolectomy Balloon Catheter</li><li>Percutaneous Thrombectomy Device</li><li>Catheter-guided Thrombolysis Device</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thrombus management device market includes various types designed to treat blood clots. Neurovascular embolectomy devices are used for removing clots from brain blood vessels. Embolectomy balloon catheters assist in both the removal and expansion of blocked arteries. Percutaneous thrombectomy devices physically extract thrombus from blood vessels via small incisions. Catheter-guided thrombolysis devices deliver thrombolytic agents directly to the clot, dissolving it effectively. Each type plays a crucial role in improving patient outcomes in vascular therapies.</p></p>
