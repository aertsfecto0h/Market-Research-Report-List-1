<p><strong>The Breast Shields Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Regular Nipple Shields</li><li>Contact Nipple Shields</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The breast shields market primarily consists of two types: regular nipple shields and contact nipple shields. Regular nipple shields are designed to assist breastfeeding by creating a barrier between the mother's nipple and the baby's mouth, helping with issues like latch problems or sore nipples. In contrast, contact nipple shields are typically thinner and designed for closer skin-to-skin contact, facilitating a more natural feeding experience while still providing some protection. Both types cater to different breastfeeding challenges and preferences.</p></p>
