<p><p>The piezoceramic actuators market consists of several types tailored for specific applications. Multilayer actuators utilize multiple layers of piezoceramics to achieve high displacement in compact designs, ideal for precise movements. Stacked actuators consist of individual layers stacked together for increased force output, suitable for applications requiring high torque. Shear actuators provide unique motion capabilities through shear deformation, offering advantages in precision applications. Amplified actuators integrate mechanical amplification to enhance output displacement, combining compact size with significant power, ideal for diverse industrial uses.</p></p>
