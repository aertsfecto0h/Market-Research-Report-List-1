<p><strong>The Acoustic Wave Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Acoustic Wave(SAW) Sensor</li><li>Bulk Acoustic Wave(BAW) Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The acoustic wave sensor market is primarily divided into two types: Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) sensors and Bulk Acoustic Wave (BAW) sensors. SAW sensors operate on the surface of a piezoelectric material, making them ideal for applications like wireless communication and environmental monitoring due to their high sensitivity and low cost. In contrast, BAW sensors utilize acoustic waves propagating through the bulk of the material, offering superior performance in high-frequency applications like medical devices and telecommunications due to their robustness and precision.</p></p>
