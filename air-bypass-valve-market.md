<p><p>The Air Bypass Valve market primarily consists of two key types: Pressure Bypass Valves and Self-operated Bypass Valves. In contrast, Self-operated Bypass Valves automatically adjust based on predetermined conditions without external power, enhancing efficiency and reliability in various applications. Both types are essential for maintaining system balance and performance in HVAC systems, industrial processes, and other air management applications.</p></p>
