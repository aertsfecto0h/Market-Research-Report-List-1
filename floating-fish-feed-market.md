<p><p>The floating fish feed market can be categorized into two main types: dry floating fish feed and wet floating fish feed. Dry floating fish feed is typically in pellet form, characterized by its buoyancy and long shelf life, making it convenient for storage and handling. It is widely used in aquaculture for various fish species. On the other hand, wet floating fish feed is moisture-rich and often includes fresh ingredients, providing better palatability and digestibility, but requires more careful handling and faster consumption.</p></p>
