<p><strong>The Carbon Black for Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Carbon Black Products</li><li>Furnace Carbon Black Products</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Carbon Black for Packaging market is primarily divided into two types: Thermal Carbon Black Products and Furnace Carbon Black Products. Thermal Carbon Black is produced using a process that involves gasification of hydrocarbons, resulting in a finer particle size and lower surface area. It enhances the conductivity and barrier properties of packaging materials. In contrast, Furnace Carbon Black is generated in a high-temperature reactor, producing larger particles with higher surface areas, making it ideal for improving strength, durability, and UV protection in packaging applications.</p></p>
