<p><strong>The Multi-lumen Tubing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PTFE</li><li>ePTFE</li><li>FEP</li><li>PFA</li><li>PEEK</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The multi-lumen tubing market comprises various types based on material composition. PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) offers chemical resistance and low friction, while ePTFE (expanded PTFE) provides enhanced flexibility and permeability. FEP (Fluorinated Ethylene Propylene) is known for its high-temperature resistance and transparency. PFA (Perfluoroalkoxy) features excellent chemical resistance and is suitable for high-purity applications. PEEK (Polyether Ether Ketone) is praised for its mechanical strength and thermal stability. Other materials may include silicone or PVC, catering to specialized applications and requirements.</p></p>
