<p><p>The heavy-duty alternators market is primarily segmented into two types: brush heavy-duty alternators and brushless heavy-duty alternators. Brush heavy-duty alternators utilize carbon brushes to transfer electrical current, making them more traditional but requiring regular maintenance due to brush wear. In contrast, brushless heavy-duty alternators eliminate the need for brushes, offering enhanced durability and reduced maintenance. These advancements cater to varying industrial needs, including construction and commercial vehicles, where reliability and performance under demanding conditions are crucial.</p></p>
