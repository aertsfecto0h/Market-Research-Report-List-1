<p><p>The food processing enzymes market comprises several types: Oxidoreductases, which facilitate oxidation-reduction reactions; Transferases, which transfer functional groups between molecules; Hydrolases, involved in the hydrolysis of bonds, breaking down complex molecules; Isomerases, which convert a molecule into its isomer; Lyases, which break chemical bonds by means other than hydrolysis or oxidation; and Ligases, which join two large molecules by forming bonds. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing food quality, flavor, preservation, and nutritional value.</p></p>
