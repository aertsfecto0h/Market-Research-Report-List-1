<p><p>The antibiotics drugs market comprises various types, each with unique mechanisms and applications. Phenicols, known for broad-spectrum activity, are used in treating serious infections. Sulfonamides inhibit bacterial growth, while aminoglycosides are effective against serious gram-negative infections. Tetracyclines are versatile in treating various infections, and beta-lactams, including penicillin, are widely utilized for their efficacy against diverse pathogens. "Others" encompasses emerging antibiotics and combinations enhancing treatment options.</p></p>
