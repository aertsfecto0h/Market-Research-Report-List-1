<p><p>The heart defects closure device market encompasses various devices used to treat specific congenital heart defects. Atrial Septal Defect (ASD) closure devices address holes between heart chambers, while Left Atrial Appendage (LAA) closure devices prevent blood clot formation. Patent Foramen Ovale (PFO) closure devices target a common heart opening present at birth. Patent Ductus Arteriosus (PDA) closure devices close an abnormal vessel, and Ventricular Septal Defect (VSD) closure devices address defects between heart ventricles. "Others" include emerging or less common devices.</p></p>
