<p><p>The PTFE lined tank market comprises primarily two types: ultrahigh purity PTFE and modified PTFE. Ultrahigh purity PTFE is designed for applications requiring minimal contamination, making it ideal for industries like pharmaceuticals and semiconductor manufacturing. It ensures high chemical resistance and low leachability. Modified PTFE, on the other hand, offers enhanced properties such as improved mechanical strength and temperature stability, making it suitable for a wider range of industrial applications, including chemical processing and storage where durability is crucial.</p></p>
