<p><p>Self-ligating suspenders are innovative orthodontic tools designed to streamline the process of dental fastening. Unlike traditional braces that require elastic or metal ligatures, self-ligating suspenders use specialized clips or brackets to hold the archwire in place, leading to improved efficiency and patient comfort. This mechanism reduces friction and allows for faster tooth movement, which can result in shorter treatment times and fewer office visits.</p><p>The self-ligating suspenders market is experiencing significant growth, driven by rising awareness of dental aesthetics and advancements in orthodontic technology.
