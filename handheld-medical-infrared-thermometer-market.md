<p><ul><li>Forehead Type Thermometer</li><li>Ear Type Thermometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld medical infrared thermometer market comprises primarily two types: forehead type and ear type thermometers. Forehead type thermometers measure body temperature by scanning the forehead, offering quick and non-invasive readings, making them ideal for children and large populations. Ear type thermometers, on the other hand, use infrared technology to detect temperature from the ear canal, providing accurate readings but requiring proper positioning for effective use. Both types are essential for efficient health monitoring, especially in clinical and home settings.</p></p>
