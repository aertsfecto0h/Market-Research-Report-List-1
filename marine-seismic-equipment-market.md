<p><strong>The Marine Seismic Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sub-Bottom Profilers</li><li>Seismic Sensors</li><li>Scalar Sensor</li><li>Vector Sensor</li><li>Geophones & Hydrophones</li><li>Streamers</li><li>Single Streamer</li><li>Multiple Streamers</li><li>Air / Water Guns</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The marine seismic equipment market encompasses various instruments essential for subsurface exploration. Sub-bottom profilers visualize seabed structures, while seismic sensors detect and measure seismic waves. Scalar sensors quantify pressure changes, and vector sensors measure directional changes in wave motion. Geophones and hydrophones capture ground and water vibrations, respectively. Streamers, both single and multiple, are towed behind vessels to gather data over large areas. Air/water guns serve as acoustic sources for generating seismic waves, facilitating geological surveys.</p></p>
