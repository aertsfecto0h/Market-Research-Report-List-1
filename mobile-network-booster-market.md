<p><ul><li>Analog Signal Booster</li><li>Digital Signal Booster</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mobile network booster market primarily comprises two types: analog signal boosters and digital signal boosters. Analog signal boosters amplify existing cellular signals and transmit them to improve coverage, often used in areas with weak reception. In contrast, digital signal boosters convert the incoming analog signal into digital form for processing, providing enhanced performance and better amplification over larger areas. Both types aim to enhance mobile connectivity and improve communication quality, catering to different user needs and network environments.</p></p>
