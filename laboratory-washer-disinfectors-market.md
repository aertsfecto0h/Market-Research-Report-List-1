<p><p>Laboratory washer-disinfectors are essential for maintaining hygiene in clinical and research settings. Cabinet (single chamber) machines are designed to sterilize and wash small to medium-sized loads, utilizing a single chamber for both washing and disinfection, making them ideal for facilities with lower throughput. In contrast, continuous process machines streamline operations by allowing for uninterrupted processing of large volumes of instruments. They utilize multiple compartments for sequential washing, rinsing, and disinfection, enhancing efficiency in high-demand environments.</p></p>
