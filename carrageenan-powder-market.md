<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Carrageenan Powder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Kappa Carrageenan</li><li>Iota Carrageenan</li><li>Lambda Carrageenan</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carrageenan powder market is segmented into three main types: Kappa, Iota, and Lambda carrageenan. Kappa carrageenan forms strong gels in the presence of potassium ions and is widely used in dairy products and meat applications. Iota carrageenan produces softer gels with calcium ions, making it suitable for puddings and sauces. Lambda carrageenan, known for its thickening properties, does not gel but stabilizes liquids, often found in dairy alternatives and beverages. Each variant caters to specific food, pharmaceutical, and cosmetic applications.</p></p>
