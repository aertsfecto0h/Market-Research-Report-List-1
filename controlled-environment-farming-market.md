<p><p>Controlled-environment farming encompasses various market types, primarily hydroponics and aeroponics, along with other methods. Hydroponics involves growing plants in a nutrient-rich water solution, minimizing soil use and maximizing yields. Aeroponics relies on misting plant roots with nutrients, enhancing oxygen exposure and growth efficiency. Other techniques include aquaponics, which combines fish farming with plant cultivation, and vertical farming, optimizing space use. These methods contribute to sustainable agriculture, ensuring year-round production while reducing resource consumption and environmental impact.</p></p>
