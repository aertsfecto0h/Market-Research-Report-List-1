<p><ul><li>Semi-rigid Barriers</li><li>Rigid Barriers</li><li>Flexible Barriers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bridge crash barrier systems market comprises three main types: semi-rigid barriers, rigid barriers, and flexible barriers. Semi-rigid barriers offer a balance of strength and deflection, allowing limited movement upon impact, which minimizes damage to vehicles and infrastructure. Rigid barriers provide maximum resistance to impact, ensuring minimal deformation but can pose higher risk to vehicles. Flexible barriers, designed to absorb impact and reduce crash forces, are less intrusive and deflect vehicles, offering enhanced safety for both drivers and pedestrians.</p></p>
